CONNECT NEWS

January 2023

In Jesus we LOVE, CONNECT, GROW

Pastor Ron Milne

Thank you for your end-of-year giving. You are very generous as a church and I know that God continues to bless you as a congregation. Somehow when you tithe and give gifts about the tithe, God makes it all work out and blesses you in unexpected ways. I hear this testimony from faithful givers over and over again. Three keys to experiencing our best year in 2023.

  • Read your Bible and pray daily;
  • Get involved in a small group Bible study;
  • By faith, do what God ask you to do;

    • This newletter will give you more details about our home group Bible study weekend with Charlie Robinson. Prayerfully plan to attend. Let Brandon Kemp know of your interest. During 2023 I will repeatedly emphasize faith and our being faithful.
    In Jesus, Pastor Ron
