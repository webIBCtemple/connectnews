In Jesus, WE LOVE, CONNECT, GROW

March 2023

Pastor Ron Milne

I have a simple challenge for you for the month of March – have a coffee or lunch with someone that you don’t know about their spiritual condition and as part of getting to know him/her ask about their religious background or where they are spiritually. Explain that you feel God wants you to pray for them at least once a week and ask if there are any specific needs for which you could be praying. Occasionally touch base to see if there are updates or to seek new prayer requests.

Our goal is to not just consider people from a physical standpoint, but to care about each one spiritually. Many people around you have no one that is regularly praying for them. I think God wants you to be that person. God wants to work through you to touch people, and God may choose to answer prayers in a specific way so that your friend will know that God is active and cares for her/him.
